BEFORE YOU START

🎧

Wear headphones or use a directional mic. The mic is always listening. Speaker sound feeding back into the mic will add unwanted colour to your painting.

HOW TO PAINT

🎵
Pitch → colour
Low hum = deep violet.
Rise in pitch = shifts through blue, cyan, green, yellow, red.
🔊
Volume → brush size
Whisper = fine delicate line.
Sing loud = broad sweeping stroke.
✋
Hand position → where
Move your hand to place the brush.
No hand = brush lifts, painting holds.
↕️
Hand height → opacity
Hand high = light, ethereal marks.
Hand low = dense, vivid strokes.
✊
Fist → slow fade
Make a fist to slowly dissolve the canvas back to black. Takes about 4 seconds. Open your hand to start painting again.
